Who We Are

Our team consists of: the Project Manager, the 3D Artist, and the Designer/Developer.

We all work together to ensure that our mission is accomplished as effectively and quickly as possible because the time left is very limited.

What Does the Timer at the Top of Each Page Tells?

That Timer is a deadline given by ClimateClock.world, it tells us that, at current rates of greenhouse gas emissions, we have less than seven years left in our global “carbon budget”.

That gives two-thirds chance of staying under the critical threshold of 1.5°C of global warming.

Technical details about the NFTs

Each individual NFT is "hand-made" with the goal of maintaining quality at the highest level possible.

They are rendered with Cycles, every object is texturized with a plugin called “BlenderKit”.

Using a crafty system of saves, every season and time of day is a separate save ensuring efficiency and a way to return to the NFT for fixes and improvements.

A collection will be released and listed for sale on opensea every 2 weeks.

Rarity Graph

The Rarity of our NFTs is really simple to understand. For each collection, the diorama comes in two editions:

Earthly seasons and premium. Each season has two versions: day (1) and night (2).

The premium edition includes gold (3) and diamond (4) version. In the future we will release the third version of the premium edition with rarity grade 5.

Where The Money Goes

The revenues from NFTs sales will be divided into two perfectly equal parts.

50% of the income will be donated for the mission, planting trees.

The remaining 50% of the money raised will be used for the maintenance, expansion and continuity of the focusedonft project, so that our help for planet earth will be as lasting as possible.

Who Will Plant Trees

We entrust the money raised and our expectations to treedom a Certified B Corporations company that has been planting trees in Africa, Latin America, Asia and Italy since 2010.

The trees will be planted every three months just like the length of one season.
